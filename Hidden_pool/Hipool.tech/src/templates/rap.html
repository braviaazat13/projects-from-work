Home

To emphasize the main events slider was located at the top of the page with news, videos and stories placed below. All scandals, versus and battles can be found in a separate section. Subscription form at the bottom of the page was added to hold the audience and keep it notified about news and events.

Discography

For music lovers we have prepared a unique functionality in the form of discography. Aseparate page with description and VC integration in the form of songs is availablefor every album.

 

Artist

For lovers of stories and biographies, we have made separate pages for artists. On these pages, user will be able to learn basic information about his / her favorite artist, photos and a short history.

Concerts

Due to the fact that this resource carries a lot of weight in media space,a page containingupcoming events with information about theirlocation, performers and time was added.
